# The game

## Game contents

1. A deck of cards with various illustrations.
   1. A golden coin
   2. A diamond
   3. The monkey & parrot
   4. Peace
   5. The skull
      1. Comes in 2 variants, either with a 1 or 2 skulls.
   6. Shipbreak
   7. The treasure chest
   8. Zombie outbreak
   9. The pirate
   10. The guard
   11. The battleship
2. A set of 8 die with 6 faces.
   1. A coin
   2. A diamond
   3. A parrot
   4. A monkey
   5. A skull
   6. A swords

## Game preparation

Shuffle the deck of cards and place them face down on the table.

## Rules

## Win condition

The first player to achieve one of either 3 rules wins;

- Reach 8000 points, every other player AFTER you gets 1 more turn.
  - If the other players manage to get your score under 8000, in which case the next person to reach 8000 instantly wins.
  - If your score is still 8000 after everyone else had their turn, then the player with the highest score NOW wins.
- Have 9 diamonds OR 9 gold on the table.

## Gameplay

A round of this game works as follows.

1. The player flips a card from the deck.
2. The player throw all 8 dice.
